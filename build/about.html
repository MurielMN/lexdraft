"Legal documents protect your family, your assets, and your rights. Everyone deserves access to them — not just those who can afford a law firm."

Our Mission

LexDraftVault provides free, AI-powered legal document generation to individuals, families, small businesses, nonprofits, and faith communities across all 50 US states. We believe that a living will, a power of attorney, or a lease agreement should be accessible to every American — regardless of income, zip code, or immigration status.

We are especially committed to serving underrepresented communities, including Spanish-speaking Americans who have historically had limited access to legal documentation in their native language. Every document on LexDraftVault is available in both English and Spanish.

How We're Different

Unlike other legal document sites that charge $40/month for a download button, LexDraftVault uses AI to generate complete, state-specific documents in real time. You describe your situation in plain English, and our AI drafts every clause, every state-specific provision, and every signature block — for free.

We cover end-of-life planning, real estate, business, personal and family, nonprofit and ministry, healthcare, and employment documents — more categories than any other free legal document service.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Nothing you enter into LexDraftVault is stored on our servers. Your documents are generated privately in your browser. We will never sell, share, or monetize your personal information.

Based in Houston, Texas

LexDraftVault is proudly based in Houston, Texas — one of the most diverse cities in America. We built this service with Houston's communities in mind: the families navigating estate planning without attorneys, the landlords and tenants who need clear lease agreements, the small business owners who need NDAs and contractor agreements, and the faith communities who need volunteer and donation documents.
